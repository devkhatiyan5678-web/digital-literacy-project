# **Professional Email & Etiquette Guide**  
In the professional world, your email is often your first impression; it signals your level of competence, respect for others' time, and attention to detail before you ever step into a room. Mastering this etiquette ensures your messages are actually read and taken seriously rather than being dismissed as unprofessional or confusing.
